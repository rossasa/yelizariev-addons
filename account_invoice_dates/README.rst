===============================================
 Start-End dates in invoice and analytic lines
===============================================

Adds fields
* Start Date
* End Date

to models:
* account.invoice.line
* account.analytic.line

Copies dates from ``account.invoice`` to ``account.analytic.line`` whenever Invoice is validated
